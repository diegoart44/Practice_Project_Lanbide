About Us

From the rough and tumble streets of New York City to the bustling hospitals where I cut my teeth as a nurse, the city's heartbeat shaped my formative years. A decade of service in the healthcare trenches immersed me in the diverse stories of its denizens.

Feeling the need for a change of pace, I traded the city's chaos for the tranquility of Bilbao's quiet mountains. The Basque Country became my refuge, a place where the clamor of urban life faded into the serene background of nature.

Join me on this unscripted journey as we navigate the skies and uncover the untold stories that drones have yet to reveal. The adventure promises to be as unpredictable as the city streets I once called home.
